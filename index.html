Creative Projects & Competitions

Games for Change (G4C) Competition

Project Theme: Climate Action Through Interactive Storytelling

Developed an educational adventure game addressing climate change awareness through engaging narrative and puzzle mechanics. The game features multiple storylines that demonstrate the interconnectedness of environmental decisions and their long-term consequences.

Innovation Highlights: Implemented dynamic story branching based on player choices, integrated real environmental data, and created accessible gameplay for diverse age groups.

Reflection: Participating in G4C challenged me to balance educational content with engaging gameplay. The feedback from judges emphasized the importance of meaningful interaction design and social impact measurement. This experience reinforced my commitment to creating games that address real-world challenges while maintaining entertainment value.

Emoticon CS Fair

Project Theme: Emotional Intelligence in Gaming

Created an innovative emotion-recognition game that uses facial expression analysis to control character abilities. The project explored the intersection of computer vision, artificial intelligence, and game design.

Technical Innovation: Integrated machine learning models for real-time emotion detection, developed adaptive difficulty based on player emotional state, and created inclusive design considerations for diverse players.

Reflection: This competition pushed me to explore emerging technologies and consider the ethical implications of emotion-based gaming. The interdisciplinary nature of the project enhanced my collaboration skills and broadened my understanding of technology's role in human expression.

InfyAPP Challenge

Project Theme: Mobile Solutions for Digital Accessibility

Developed a mobile application focusing on accessibility in gaming, featuring customizable controls, visual/audio assistance options, and inclusive design principles for players with diverse abilities.

Impact Focus: Researched accessibility standards, implemented universal design principles, and collaborated with accessibility advocacy groups for authentic user feedback.

Reflection: The InfyAPP Challenge opened my perspective on inclusive design and the responsibility of developers to create accessible experiences. Working with the disability community taught me valuable lessons about user-centered design and the importance of diverse testing groups.
